Bidding Rules:

  • Bidding will commence at 3pm.
  • Every participant will be provided with the same number of E-Coins which will allow them to bid on companies.
  • Participants will openly bid for companies to buy at the lowest rate possible.
  • Every participant is allowed to buy only one company.
  • Companies need to be bought as a whole entity and not in equities.
  • Once a participant has won a bid he/she will be refrained from any further bidding.

Trading Rules:

  • Trading will commence at 12pm.
  • Every hour E-coins will be added to participants' wallets equal to 1000 multiplied by the multiplication factor.
  • There will be 3 news flashes every hour which may or maynot affect the total value of a particular company and thus can be traded accordingly.
  • Subtle hints will be spread across all our channels. Crack them before others to get a nudge on the upcoming calamity or even a profitable trade idea.
